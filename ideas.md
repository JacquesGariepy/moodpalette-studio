# moodpalette-studio - Ideas & Artistic Direction

## Concept
Aether est un dashboard personnel ultra-premium qui regroupe toute ta vie en un seul endroit :

Agenda intelligent avec vue calendrier + timeline
Notes AI (comme Notion mais plus beau)
Habit tracker avec streaks visuels
Finance overview (solde, dépenses par catégorie, graphiques)
Weather + mood journal
Section “Focus Mode” avec timer Pomodoro + musique ambient
Sidebar gauche fixe avec tous les modules
Top bar avec recherche globale + avatar + quick actions

Le tout dans une interface dark glassmorphism très fluide, avec des micro-animations subtiles, des cartes qui “lèvent” au hover, des gradients doux et des illustrations 3D légères (via Lottie ou simple SVG animés).

## Structure
App Type: Personal Dashboard
Design: Aether embodies ultra-premium dark glassmorphism inspired by Arc Browser, Linear.app, and Apple Vision Pro interfaces. The design features a sophisticated dark color palette (charcoal blacks #0a0a0a, deep grays #1a1a1a, #2a2a2a) with frosted glass cards using backdrop-blur effects and subtle border luminosity. Soft purple-to-blue gradients (#8b5cf6 to #3b82f6) accent interactive elements. Every card floats with gentle shadow elevation and lifts smoothly on hover via transform transitions. Micro-animations breathe life into state changes—habit streaks pulse with success, finance charts animate on load, and the Pomodoro timer features a fluid circular progress ring. 3D depth is achieved through layered glassmorphic panels, subtle parallax scrolling, and animated SVG illustrations. Typography is clean and modern (Inter or SF Pro), with generous whitespace ensuring premium readability. The fixed sidebar uses frosted acrylic texture, while the top search bar features an elegant spotlight effect. The entire interface prioritizes fluid 60fps animations, creating the most visually stunning personal dashboard of 2026.
